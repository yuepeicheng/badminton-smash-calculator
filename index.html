How it works (math)
  • Click two calibration points with known real-world separation L meters → compute meters-per-pixel.
  • Mark shuttle at time t₁ and t₂; measure pixel distance dpx between clicks.
  • Distance in meters: d = dpx × (L / calib_px).
  • Time difference: Δt = |t₂ − t₁| (from the video timestamp).
  • Raw speed: v = d / Δt. Skew-corrected: v' = v / cos(θ).
